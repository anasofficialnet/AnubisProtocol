I write these words in haste, for the sands are shifting and the tomb
will soon be sealed forever. The Protocol of Anubis was designed to
guard the deepest chamber — the Sanctum — from all who would seek to
plunder its secrets.

The Final Gateway demands the True Name. It was whispered to me by
the High Priestess on the night of my initiation, when I knelt before
the obsidian scales and swore my eternal oath.

She spoke: "The first seal of the True Name was given to me alone.
I inscribe it here so that it may survive the ages:

    Kh4s3m_Th3_3t3rn4l

The second seal is carved upon the walls of the Inner Sanctum itself.
Only those who gaze upon the ancient stone with their own eyes shall
read what was written there. No scribe can copy it — the glyphs
resist all but direct sight."

After speaking both seals as one, the Gateway will demand proof
of your station. Only a true servant of the Order may pass.

May Osiris judge you worthy.

                                        — Khasem, 7th Scribe
